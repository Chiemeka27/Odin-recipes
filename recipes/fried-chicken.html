Fried Chicken

Description

We have determined the nutritional value of oil for frying based on a retention value of 10% after cooking. The exact amount may vary depending on cook time and temperature, ingredient density, and the specific type of oil used.

Per Serving: 623 calories; protein 38.9g; carbohydrates 21.9g; fat 41.3g; cholesterol 114.9mg; sodium 107.7mg.

ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Salt and pepper chicken pieces to taste
  3. roll in flour to coat.
  4. Place chicken pieces in skillet and fry on medium heat until one side is golden brown
  5. turn and brown other side until chicken is no longer pink inside
  6. Drain on paper towel and serve
